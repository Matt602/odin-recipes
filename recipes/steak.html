Steak

Description

Learn how to cook a delicious steak that is searing hot and juicy.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Sprig of rosemary or thyme
  • A third of a stick of butter
  • 2 or 3 garlic cloves
  • Vegetable or canola oil
  • Thick cut of ribeye steak(preferably choice or prime grade)

Steps

  1. Take the steak out of refrigerator for 10 minutes before cooking
  2. Season both sides of steak with a dash of salt and some black pepper
  3. Grab a cast iron skillet or stainless steel pan
  4. Turn on stove to about between medium and high heat
  5. Put pan on heat until it starts to smoke. So about 5 minutes
  6. Add oil to pan
  7. Immediately add the steak on to pan
  8. After about 4 minutes, grab a set of tongs and place the steak on the pan so that the fat strip of the steak can be rendered down. Hold it for about 2 minutes.
  9. Then place the uncooked side of the steak on the pan for another 4 minutes
  10. Drain the oil from the pan.
  11. Turn the stove to low heat.
  12. Add the butter, rosemary, and garlic cloves to pan. Wait about 30 seconds.
  13. Put the rosemary, or thyme, and garlic on top of steak.
  14. Then grab a spoon and carefully tilt the pan to allow melted butter to collect to a side of the pan. Start pouring the butter on to steak to baste it. Do this for about 3 minutes.
  15. Finally, turn the stove off, take the steak off the pan, and let it rest for about 10 minutes before eating.
